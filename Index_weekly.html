Rainfall measurement across Pune City - Citizens Voluntary Initiative
Purpose: Started out of curiosity to know 'how much it rains in My area', the initiave is now extended to record rainfall patterns in adjucent areas as well as across Pune city.
Procedure : Rain gauges used are either home made or are brought from market. Rainfall is measured at 8:30am everyday for 24 Hr span and centralised record of all data points is maintained.
Measurement of 2.5mm or less at 3 or less locations is considered as 'No Rainfall'
Call for Volunteers - Interested Citizens especially in the areas around Pune East (Hadarpsar, Phursungi, Wanawadi) are welcome to paticipate in this initiative. Contact Abhijit Gandhi (98228-75550) for any feedback
